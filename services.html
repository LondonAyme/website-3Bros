Our Services

Reliable support for busy families and relatable guidance for students. We combine academic excellence with a friendly, local approach.

Tutoring

Our approach focuses on clear explanations, mastering exam techniques, and building long-term confidence. We've been in your shoes recently and know exactly what examiners are looking for.

Subjects Covered:

  • Maths & Physics: Specialised support for the French Baccalaureate and GCSE/A-Levels.
  • French: Native-level fluency for all levels (Primary to Adult).
  • Coding: Introduction to Python, MATLAB, and web basics.

For Parents: We provide structured progress reports and focus on academic results.

For Students: No boring lectures – just clear help from someone who understands the pressure.

Babysitting

Safe, reliable care for children aged 5 to 15. Whether it's weekend coverage or evening support, we ensure your children are safe, happy, and engaged.

  • Available evenings and weekends
  • DBS-checked & fully insured
  • Experience with a wide range of ages
  • Active engagement (games, homework help, outdoors)

For Parents: Peace of mind knowing your kids are with responsible, local university students.

Pet Care

We provide trustworthy care for your furry family members. From daily walks to holiday feeding visits, we treat every pet with the same love and attention as our own.

  • Dog walking & exercise
  • Home feeding visits for cats & small pets
  • Weekend/Holiday pet sitting
  • Photo/Text updates after every visit

For Parents: Reliable care from animal lovers who understand the importance of your pet's routine.
